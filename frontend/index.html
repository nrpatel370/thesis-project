Configuration

2

Categories optional

Customize how column names are matched into grade buckets. Each keyword is matched case-insensitively anywhere in the column name. Defaults cover most standard Canvas exports. (Main categories used are Labs, Debug Dungeon, Attendance and Final Exam)

3

Formula Weights optional

Adjust the 8 calculation weights for your course: lab weight, Debug Dungeon weight, multipliers & fallback denominators. Any field left blank uses the built-in default.

5

Column Selection

After uploading, review the auto-categorized columns. Check the Labs and Debug Dungeon columns to include, then pick Attendance and Final Exam from the dropdowns. Click Save & Calculate when ready.
